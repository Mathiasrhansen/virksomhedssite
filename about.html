ABOUT

  • Our training is simple, challenging, effective - and fun. No mirrors. No machines. No gimmicks. You don't need to bring much: just yourself, a mat and a bottle of water. We don't rely on equipment - YOU are the machine.

    Whether you're a beginner or advanced, all levels are welcome. If you're ready to try and want to improve, we promise: You'll get better.

    Join us weekly in the city, or for free weekend runs. Create your profile. Book your sessions. Move differently.

  • We're city people, but we love the outdoors. That's why we train outside all year - rain, wind, snow or sun. No excuses.

    In todays society most people stay inside and sit down the majority of the day. We do the exact opposite of that. By moving the physical activity outdoor we get fresh air, light, sun and dirt under our nails. Just like when we were kids. We do it because it feels good, it clears the mind - and because we enjoy it.
